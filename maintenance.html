Regular Maintenance Schedule

  • Change engine oil and filter every 3,000 miles or as recommended in your owner's manual.
  • Inspect and replace air filters as needed.
  • Check and maintain tire pressure.
  • Inspect and replace brake pads and rotors according to the manufacturer's recommendations.
  • Perform regular fluid checks, including coolant, brake fluid, and transmission fluid.
  • Inspect the suspension system for wear and tear.
  • Verify the functionality of lights, including headlights, taillights, and turn signals.

Specialized Maintenance

Koenigsegg cars are high-performance vehicles with unique engineering. In addition to regular maintenance, it is recommended to schedule specialized maintenance with authorized Koenigsegg service centers. This may include advanced diagnostics, software updates, and thorough inspections by trained technicians.

Emergency Maintenance

In the event of a breakdown or emergency, contact your nearest Koenigsegg service center for assistance. Follow the guidelines provided in your owner's manual for emergency procedures and contact information.
