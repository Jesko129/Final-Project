Koenigsegg, a pioneer in automotive excellence, was founded in 1994 by Christian von Koenigsegg. This Swedish automaker is renowned for pushing the limits of innovation, creating hypercars that blend stunning design with unparalleled performance.

At Koenigsegg, innovation is at the forefront. The company challenges conventions, constantly redefining the automotive landscape. Their hypercars, featuring iconic design elements like dihedral synchro-helix actuation doors, embody aerodynamic mastery.

Under the hood, Koenigsegg's cutting-edge, in-house engines, including the revolutionary Freevalve technology, exemplify high-performance engineering. The brand has set records for speed, with hypercars like the Jesko Absolut pushing boundaries and showcasing dedication to performance.

Inside a Koenigsegg, luxury meets cutting-edge technology, providing a captivating driving experience. Limited production runs make each hypercar a collector's dream, attracting enthusiasts globally. Koenigsegg's legacy as an innovator in the automotive industry continues to thrive, marking its place as a symbol of excellence.
